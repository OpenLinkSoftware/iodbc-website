In the interests of providing a higher-quality forum for iODBC users and developers, we have created several mailing-lists hosted on SourceForge.net, for various general topics of discussion.

These are:

  • iodbc-announce, for announcements we make about new versions, or other important news.
  • iodbc-applications, for using iODBC with various applications (e.g. scripting languages, etc)
  • iodbc-bugs, for discussion of bugs found in iODBC
  • iodbc-list, for miscellaneous iODBC-related discussion
  • iodbc-macosx, for discussion of iODBC on the MacOS X platform
