iODBC is purely an ODBC Driver Manager, not an ODBC driver. The following sites provide links to ODBC drivers for common databases.

  • OpenLink Software home
  • OpenLink product download
  • Ken North's catalogues of ODBC drivers, .Net providers, OleDB providers and JDBC drivers
  • A list of free ODBC drivers and database tools on TheFreeCountry.com
  • MySQL's ODBC driver download
